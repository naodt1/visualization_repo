### Task 3: How

#### Explain your design decision
We chose a bar chart for comparing team performance in athletics because it uses the most effective visual channels for a combination of categorical (Teams) and quantitative data (Total number of medals). Scatter plot, on the other hand, is best used when we want to encode two quantitative attributes, for example, height and weight. Following are the marks and channels in our plot:

* **Mark**: Line which is represented by a rectangular bar. Each bar is a unique team.
* **Channel**: The vertical position on a common scale. Higher vertical position means higher medal count.

The common baseline at zero enables accurate magnitude comparison through postioning using a common scale. This is the most effective channel for ordered attributes.
