### Task 1: What

#### Dataset type:
The dataset type is a "Table" consisting of Attributes and Items.

#### Attribute types:
1. Name - Categorical (Edgar Lindenau Aabye, Christine Jacoba Aaftink, Jyri Tapani Aalto)
2. Sex - Categorical (Male or Female)
3. Age - Quantitative
4. Height - Quantitative 
5. Weight - Quantitative
6. Team - Categorical (Examples: China, Denmark, Netherlands)
7. NCO - Categorical (Examples: DEN, NED, USA)
8. Games - Categorical (Examples: 1988 Winter, 1992 Winter, 2002 Winter)
9. Year - Ordinal (From: 1952 to 2016 with intervals)
10. Season - Categorical (Examples: Summer, Winter)
11. City - Categorical (Examples: Barcelona, London, Paris, Albertville)
12. Sport - Categorical (Speed Skating, Basketball, Judo, Swimming)
13. Event - Categorical (Examples: Speed Skating Women's 500 metres, Cross Country Skiing Men's 10 kilometres, Cross Country Skiing Men's 30 kilometres)
14. Medal - Ordinal (From: Bronze, Gold, Silver)
